# GuardianRisk

GuardianRisk is a cybersecurity risk assessment and governance platform that automates organizational risk evaluation using NIST SP 800-30 methodologies.

## Overview

The platform helps organizations:

- Identify critical assets
- Assess cybersecurity threats
- Calculate risk scores
- Visualize security posture
- Generate mitigation-focused insights

GuardianRisk simplifies complex GRC workflows into an intuitive dashboard for technical and non-technical stakeholders.

---

## Features

### Risk Assessment Engine
- Likelihood × Impact risk scoring
- Automated risk categorization
- Threat mapping workflows

### Dashboard & Analytics
- Risk heatmaps
- Security readiness visualization
- Critical asset tracking

### Asset Management
- Centralized asset inventory
- Business impact classification
- Risk prioritization

### Reporting
- Executive-style summaries
- Compliance-oriented insights
- Mitigation recommendations

---

## Risk Formula

Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact

| Score | Severity |
|---|---|
| 1–5 | Low |
| 6–10 | Medium |
| 11–15 | High |
| 16–25 | Critical |
